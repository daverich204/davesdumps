Who is sitting behind the wheel?

It all started with a dream...

Growing tired in the field of Software Development, Chef D decided to embark on a new quest, a quest to open the best dumpling truck in town.

With a supportive family, and growing fan base, Chef D invested in an idea, an idea that great dumplings should be affordable and attainable for all.

Chef D is working on revolutionizing the food truck industry, think Skip the Dishes, but the food is made as it drives to your house.
